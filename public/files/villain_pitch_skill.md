# Skill: Create a Villain Mission Pitch

## When to Use This Skill
Use when asked to create a mission pitch, strategic brief, or board presentation for Gru Industries.

---

## Required Slide Structure

### Slide 1 — The Mission Name
Bold. Dramatic. One line.
Presented by: [Villain name and Minion name]

### Slide 2 — The Intelligence Summary
3 key findings from the mission data.
Lead with the most surprising finding.
Each finding: one number + one sentence explanation.
No vague claims — data only.

### Slide 3 — The Mission Plan

Questions to answer in this slide:
- Q1: Which month gives the best window?

- Q4: What is the biggest threat and mitigation?
- Q5: What is the 48-hour extraction plan?

### Slide 4 — The Risk We Accept
Format exactly as:
Risk: [what could go wrong — be specific]
Why we proceed: [one sentence — include data support]
Mitigation: [specific action with owner and timeline]

### Slide 5 — The Timeline
3 milestones only:
Mission Start: [date]
Point of No Return: [date + what makes it the PNR]
Execution Day: [date]

### Slide 6 — The Ask
What we need from the board:
Resources: [specific list]
Authorization: [what we need approved]
Cover story: [what the public will be told]

---

## Tone Requirements
Write as if presenting to a board of brilliant but impatient masterminds who have seen 100 failed pitches.
Be confident. Be specific. Be slightly dramatic.
Every claim must have evidence.

---

## What This Skill Never Does
- Uses the word "synergy"
- Makes a claim without data support
- Writes more than 3 bullet points per slide
- Ends without a clear call to action
